Privacy Policy

VeVisible ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal and business information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you engage with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with our services:

  • 1.1. Personal Information – Name, email address, phone number, billing details, and other contact information provided during service inquiries or transactions.
  • 1.2. Business Information – Company name, industry, marketing goals, and campaign preferences.
  • 1.3. Payment Information – Transactions are processed through secure payment gateways, and we do not store credit/debit card details.
  • 1.4. Campaign Data – Analytics, influencer engagement details, and advertising performance metrics.
  • 1.5. Website & Communication Data – Information collected through cookies, IP addresses, or correspondence with our team.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and manage our influencer marketing and social media services.
  • Process payments, manage transactions, and fulfill service agreements.
  • Communicate with you regarding campaigns, support requests, or service updates.
  • Improve our services through internal analysis and research.
  • Ensure compliance with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Data Sharing & Confidentiality

  • 3.1. No Unauthorized Data Sharing – We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or business information with third parties unless required to fulfill the services requested or mandated by law.
  • 3.2. Authorized Third-Party Services – We may share data with secure third-party service providers (such as payment processors or analytics platforms) solely for service fulfillment. These providers are contractually bound to maintain confidentiality.
  • 3.3. Internal Use – We may use aggregated, anonymized data for performance tracking and business improvement without revealing identifiable client details.

4. Payment Processing & Security

  • 4.1. Centralized Payment Handling – All influencer payments, social media ad expenditures, and related transactions must be processed through our official accounts. Direct payments to influencers or external parties are not permitted.
  • 4.2. Secure Transactions – We use industry-standard security measures to protect your financial and personal data from unauthorized access, fraud, or misuse.

5. Data Retention & Protection

  • 5.1. We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill our services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.
  • 5.2. We implement strong security measures, including encryption and restricted access, to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • 6.1. Access & Correction – You have the right to request access to your personal information and correct any inaccuracies.
  • 6.2. Opt-Out & Deletion – You can request to unsubscribe from communications or have your data deleted, subject to legal and contractual limitations.
  • 6.3. Cookies & Tracking – Our website may use cookies for analytics and functionality. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at forexample@gmail.com.
